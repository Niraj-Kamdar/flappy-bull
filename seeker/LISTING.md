```
Flappy Bull turns the live SOL/USD chart into an arcade obstacle course. Tap
to fly the bull through the gap between price candles — the market decides
where the walls are. Every run streams on-chain in real time on Solana,
powered by MagicBlock Ephemeral Rollups for instant, gasless taps. When you
die, your score commits permanently to a shared, real-time leaderboard.
Spectate live runs, climb the Hall of Degens, and dethrone the bull whisperers.

- Real-time on-chain gameplay via MagicBlock Ephemeral Rollups

- One signature to start; taps are gasless after that

Not financial advice. SOL/USD data used for gameplay only.
```
